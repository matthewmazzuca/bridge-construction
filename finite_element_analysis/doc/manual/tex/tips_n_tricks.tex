This chapter is here to list the tips and tricks related to \feel.

\section{Logs}
You can find your logs in /tmp/"username"/feelpp/"app_name"/\ldots

\section{Results}
You will find your results in $HOME/feel/"app_name"/\cdots
You may want to write elsewhere, the simplest way to do so is to create a simlink to your prefered directory.

\section{options}
to list all the options provided by your application, juste type:
./app_name --help

It is recommended to pipe it with less, ie:
./app_name --help | less
